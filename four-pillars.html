The Four Pillars Diagnostic™

Every broken business fails
in the same four places.

40 weighted factors across 4 pillars. Each scored 0–3 based on severity. Higher score = worse problem. This is the engine that drives every OpFix engagement.

⚙️ Pillar 1

Operations & Systems

The infrastructure that keeps your business running without you. If these break, nothing else matters.

SOP Documentation
Weight: 3x
Are your core processes documented and executable by anyone on the team?
Tech Stack Integration
Weight: 2x
Is your tech stack connected with a single source of truth, or fragmented across tools?
Workflow Consistency
Weight: 3x
Does every team member follow the same process, or does each client get a different experience?
Reporting & KPI Tracking
Weight: 2x
Can you see your business health in 60 seconds, or are you making gut-feel decisions?
Bottleneck & Dependency
Weight: 3x
How many decisions require you personally? How long does the business last without you?
Quality Control Systems
Weight: 2x
Are errors caught proactively by systems, or reactively when clients complain?
Client Delivery Process
Weight: 3x
Is delivery mapped with phase gates and sign-offs, or improvised every time?
Internal Communication
Weight: 1x
One platform with SLAs, or scattered across text, email, and DMs?
Project Management
Weight: 2x
Is every task tracked, assigned, and deadlined — or managed by memory?
Business Continuity
Weight: 3x
Can the business run 2+ weeks without you, or does it collapse in 48 hours?
Max pillar severity: 72 pts
📈 Pillar 2

Revenue & Sales

The engine that generates, converts, and retains revenue. If this pillar bleeds, growth is impossible.

Lead Generation System
Weight: 3x
Multi-channel with consistent flow, or pipeline empty unless you hustle?
Sales Process Documentation
Weight: 3x
End-to-end with scripts and stages, or each rep selling differently?
Conversion Tracking
Weight: 2x
Stage-by-stage tracking with targets, or no idea where leads drop?
Follow-Up & Nurture
Weight: 2x
Automated sequences per stage, or one touch then gone forever?
Offer Clarity & Differentiation
Weight: 3x
Named, packaged, and crystal clear — or vague and competing on price?
Pricing Strategy
Weight: 3x
Value-based with tiers, or made up per deal in a race to the bottom?
Revenue Concentration
Weight: 2x
Diversified across 10+ clients, or top 3 clients are 70% of revenue?
Client Retention & Churn
Weight: 2x
Proactive retention system, or constant need for new clients?
Upsell / Cross-Sell
Weight: 1x
Automated triggers with ascension model, or no upsell awareness?
Marketing ROI Tracking
Weight: 2x
Every dollar tracked with attribution, or spending with zero visibility?
Max pillar severity: 69 pts
💰 Pillar 3

Financial Structure

The numbers that determine whether you're building wealth or burning it. Most founders don't know these are broken until it's too late.

Profit Margin Visibility
Weight: 3x
Known per service line and reviewed monthly, or no idea what real margin is?
Cash Flow Predictability
Weight: 3x
13-week forecast at 90%+ accuracy, or invoice to invoice?
Expense Discipline
Weight: 2x
Monthly audit with approval policy, or subscriptions piling up unmanaged?
Debt Structure
Weight: 2x
No debt or fully manageable, or toxic MCAs consuming cash flow?
Financial Forecasting
Weight: 2x
12-month with 3 scenarios, or month to month with zero visibility?
Pricing Model Effectiveness
Weight: 3x
Value-based with formula, or 30-50% of revenue left on the table?
Revenue Model Sustainability
Weight: 2x
20%+ recurring with ladder, or every month starts at zero?
Funding Readiness
Weight: 1x
Business credit established, or personally guaranteeing everything?
Invoicing & Collections
Weight: 2x
Automated with 90%+ collected in 7 days, or chaotic with significant AR?
Financial Reporting Cadence
Weight: 2x
Monthly close by the 10th with dashboard, or only at tax time?
Max pillar severity: 66 pts
👥 Pillar 4

Team & Leadership

The people structure that makes everything else scalable. Without this pillar, every fix you install is temporary.

Org Chart & Role Clarity
Weight: 3x
Every role defined with KPIs and lanes, or chaos where nobody owns anything?
Talent Quality in Key Seats
Weight: 3x
A-players in every critical role, or wrong people and top performers leaving?
Performance Metrics Per Role
Weight: 2x
2-3 KPIs per role with targets, or no way to know who performs?
Delegation Maturity
Weight: 3x
Team empowered with 80%+ of your time on strategy, or you do everything?
Hiring Pipeline & Process
Weight: 2x
Full SOP with structured interviews, or desperate hires when someone quits?
Onboarding & Training
Weight: 2x
Day 1-30 program with modules and milestones, or thrown in to figure it out?
Accountability Framework
Weight: 2x
Weekly standups, 1-on-1s, and defined consequences, or honor system?
Culture & Retention
Weight: 1x
Values lived with high engagement, or toxic with good people leaving?
Leadership Development
Weight: 1x
Development plans with succession path, or you're the only leader?
Succession Planning
Weight: 2x
Every function mapped with a successor, or business collapses without you?
Max pillar severity: 63 pts
